Duck Pond Campsites Rulebook

Welcome to Duck Pond Campsites! To ensure a safe, enjoyable, and harmonious camping experience for all our guests, we kindly ask that you review and abide by the following rules and guidelines:

For furthur information on wildlife best practices, visit the Kansas Department of Wildlife and Parks.

1. Campsite Reservations:

  • All campsites must be reserved in advance through our online booking system.
  • Campers should display their reservation confirmation visibly at their campsite.

2. Check-In and Check-Out:

  • Check-in time is from 2:00 PM onwards.
  • Check-out time is by 12:00 PM.
  • Please adhere to these times to allow our team to prepare the sites for incoming guests.

3. Quiet Hours:

  • Respect quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM to ensure a peaceful environment for all campers.

4. Campfires:

  • Fires are only allowed in designated fire pits.
  • Follow local fire regulations and never leave a fire unattended.
  • Extinguish fires completely before leaving your campsite.

5. Waste Management:

  • Dispose of all trash in designated bins.
  • Practice Leave No Trace principles. Pack out what you pack in.

6. Pets:

  • Well-behaved pets are welcome but must be kept on a leash at all times.
  • Clean up after your pets.

7. Wildlife Interaction:

  • Observe wildlife from a distance. Do not feed or approach wild animals.

8. Fishing:

  • Fishing is allowed in designated areas.
  • Follow catch-and-release guidelines to preserve our ecosystem.

9. Respect for Nature:

  • Stay on marked trails and respect natural habitats.
  • Do not disturb plant life or wildlife.

10. Facilities Usage:

  • Respect posted rules for restroom and shower facilities.
  • Do not wash dishes or clothes in bathroom sinks.

11. Emergency Procedures:

  • Familiarize yourself with emergency exits and procedures.
  • In case of emergency, contact campsite management immediately.

12. Alcohol and Substance Use:

  • Alcohol consumption is permitted in moderation.
  • The use of illegal substances is strictly prohibited.

13. Bicycles and Vehicles:

  • Observe speed limits for bicycles and vehicles within the campsite.
  • Only authorized vehicles are allowed in designated areas.

14. Community Courtesy:

  • Be considerate of fellow campers. Keep noise levels to a minimum.
  • Report any disruptive behavior to campsite management.

15. Campsite Etiquette:

  • Respect the boundaries of neighboring campsites.
  • Follow any additional rules posted at specific campsites.

Failure to comply with these rules may result in eviction from the campsite without a refund. Thank you for your cooperation in making Duck Pond Campsites a wonderful and respectful community for all campers. We hope you enjoy your stay in the great outdoors!
